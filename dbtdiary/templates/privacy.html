Privacy Policy

Website Visitors

Like most website operators, DBTDiary.com collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. DBTDiary.com’s purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how DBTDiary.com’s visitors use its website. From time to time, DBTDiary.com may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.

DBTDiary.com also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses. DBTDiary.com does not use such information to identify its visitors, however, and does not disclose such information, other than under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information, as described below.

Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information

Certain visitors to DBTDiary.com’s websites choose to interact with DBTDiary.com in ways that require DBTDiary.com to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that DBTDiary.com gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who create diary cards to provide a username. In each case, DBTDiary.com collects such information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor’s interaction with DBTDiary.com. DBTDiary.com does not disclose personally-identifying information other than as described below. And visitors can always refuse to supply personally-identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.

DBTDiary.com discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only to those of its employees, contractors, and affiliated organizations that (i) need to know that information in order to process it on DBTDiary.com’s behalf or to provide services available at DBTDiary.com’s website, and (ii) that have agreed not to disclose it to others. Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. DBTDiary.com uses cookies to help DBTDiary.com identify and track visitors, their usage of DBTDiary.com website, and their website access preferences. DBTDiary.com visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using DBTDiary.com’s website, with the drawback that certain features of DBTDiary.com’s website may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

Although most changes are likely to be minor, DBTDiary.com may change its Privacy Policy from time to time, and in DBTDiary.com’s sole discretion. Your continued use of this site after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.
